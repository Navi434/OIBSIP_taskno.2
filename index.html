BTS - Beyond The Stars

A South Korean Boyband who is spreading love and happiness all around the world

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; RR: Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. There are seven members in the group- Kim Namjoon(RM),Kim Seokjin (Jin), Min Yoongi (Suga/ Agust D), Jung Hoseok (J-Hope), Park Jimin(Jimin), Kim Taehyung (V), Jeon Jungkook (JK). BTS is a group of 3 rappers and 4 vocalists known for their stage performances, lyricism and love for thier fans called ARMY. Together they've won over 700 awards including Artist of the year at 2021 American Music Awards and 12 Billboard Music Awards. BTS have the fastest accumulation of 6 #1 songs on Billboard Hot100. The Leader of this group is RM i.e Kim Namjoon. They Co-writes, C0-Produces their songs on their own. Originally BTS is known as a hip hop group but their musical style has evolved to include a wide range of genres. Their lyrics touch on the themes of mental health, depression, social justice, soceital expectations, troubles of school-age, youth , loss, self love, and individualism. Their work also often references literature and psychological concepts and includes an alternative universe storyline.

Their incredible fanbase named 'ARMY' has been an immense factor in gaining the group the unprecendented success that they have experienced and the boys never fail to acknowledge that. Through their positive and relatable message, the boys of BTS have been able to connect with millions of people around the globe. Combined with their amazing music, visuals, message, social media interactions as well as numerous contents, BTS have managed to grow their fandom into a formidable 'ARMY' and the fandom continues to grow as BTS gets more main stream exposure in the western market.
